About The LinAlg Toolkit

The LinAlg Toolkit is a quick helper for linear algebra! Here's what it does so far:

  • RREFs matrices
  • Row, column, and null spaces
  • Transposes
  • Determinants
  • Inverse matrices
  • Polar-Rectangular form conversion for complex numbers
  • Multiplication and division of complex numbers in rectangular form

Built by Deon Hua, a Software Engineering student at the University of Waterloo. You can find this project on GitHub.

Developed with AngularJS, MathJax, Flask, Sympy, and Python.

Made with love.
